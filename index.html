I love discovering all the possible solutions to a problem and then figuring out the best fit.

Hello, I am Kristen Wilcox, a UX/UI design student and high school art teacher. I specialize in ideation. I tell my art students that there is no right answer when creating art and the beauty is in all the possibilities. I remind them that they can grow their skills in judgement to find the best fit for them and learn to find their ‘voice’.

I know that I will bring this philosophy to any team I work with and to any design. Although I am a natural divergent thinker, I enjoy the process of making an idea attainable and relevant. I am open to feedback and collaboration and really love working with others to problem-solve.
